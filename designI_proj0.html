Design I Project 0

This project was for my Design I for Media class. It was an introductory assignment to Adobe Illustrator. We were given two triangles, two squares, and a circle, and we had to create a certain number of small compositions with different forms of symmetry. This assignment was a little challenging because it was difficult to come up with 24 different compositions while keeping them imtresting, but I am proud of the final product.
